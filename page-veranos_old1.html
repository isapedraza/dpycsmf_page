Purpose

The purpose of this competition is to select Mexican undergraduate and master's students as candidates for summer research stays at international research centers, to strengthen their training in particle physics, astroparticle physics, accelerators, and related areas.

⚠️ The DPyC does not guarantee that the host institution will accept the preselected students. Candidates are encouraged to carefully review the competition terms before applying.

About the stay

During the summer research stay, students join an international laboratory and participate in academic and research activities designed to strengthen their training. Over approximately two months, students work under the guidance of researchers and integrate into the host laboratory's daily life.

📚

Short, intensive courses — theory, experimental methods, data analysis, detectors, and simulation.

🔬

Supervised research project: data analysis, simulation, detector studies, or theoretical calculations.

🎤

Seminars, group meetings, and scientific discussions in active international research environments.

💻

Collaborative work with research teams — technical, computational, and scientific skills.

🏛

Laboratory visits, technical tours, and hands-on sessions depending on the host center's program.

🎓

Final presentation or summary of work — valuable experience for academic and professional development.

Selection process
1

Review of academic profile

The committee evaluates submitted documents — academic transcripts, reference letters, motivation letters, and CVs — to assess academic preparation, research potential, and alignment with the program's objectives.

2

In-person evaluation of practical skills

Applicants who advance participate in an in-person assessment including short intensive courses, problem-solving activities, and an interview. This evaluates analytical abilities, scientific reasoning, communication skills, and overall suitability for the stay. In 2026 this took place at UAEH, Pachuca, January 19–21.

Financial coverage

The host institution partially funds the research stay. Depending on the host, support may include:

✈️

Airfare

🏠

Lodging

💳

Stipend

🍽

Subsistence support

🏥

Health insurance

Once selected, the candidate commits to requesting any additional funding needed from their home institution. The DPyC supports, with documentation, the process for securing complementary funding to complete the research stay.
